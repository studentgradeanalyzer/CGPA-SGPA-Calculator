What is SGPA?

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is calculated using grades and credits for subjects in one semester.

Formula: SGPA = Σ (Credit × Grade Point) / Total Credits

What is CGPA?

CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) is the average of all SGPAs across semesters.

Formula: CGPA = Σ (SGPA × Credit) / Σ Credits (if known), otherwise CGPA = Average of SGPAs

Conversion to Percentage

General/CBSE: CGPA × 9.5
Delhi University: (CGPA - 0.5) × 10
Central Universities: CGPA × 9.5
